Apple Omelette

Description

A perfect combination of sweet, tart, and savory, the apple omelette can be as simple as scrambling two key ingredients, but is always an unexpected delight.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg, whisked
  • 1 apple, minced
  • Cinammon
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. After mincing the apple and whisking the egg, season each with cinnamon and sugar to taste. Sprinkle some salt into the egg as well.
  2. Heat up olive oil in the pan and add in the apple slices. Then, sautee the apple for 3-5 minutes until the apple is soft.
  3. Make sure the apple is spread out evenly, then pour the whisked egg evenly over the pan. Cook them together until the egg is at desired doneness.
